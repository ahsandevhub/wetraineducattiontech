# Landing Module Documentation

## Overview

The Landing module provides the public-facing marketing website for WeTrainEducation & Tech.

## Business Purpose

- **Target Users**: Potential customers and visitors
- **Value Proposition**: Showcase services, courses, and company capabilities
- **Conversion Focus**: Drive signups, inquiries, and sales

## Routes & Pages

### Public Routes (`/(landing)/*`)

- `/` - Homepage with hero and services
- `/about` - Company information
- `/courses` - Course catalog
- `/services` - Service offerings
- `/software` - Software products
- `/contact` - Contact forms
- `/privacy` - Privacy policy
- `/terms` - Terms of service
- `/refund` - Refund policy
- `/checkout` - Course purchase flow
- `/login` - Authentication entry
- `/register` - User registration
- `/verify` - Email verification
- `/marketing` - Marketing service pages

## Business Workflows

### Lead Generation Flow

1. Visitor lands on marketing pages
2. Engages with content and CTAs
3. Submits contact/lead forms
4. Data captured for CRM follow-up
5. Conversion tracking

### Course Sales Flow

1. Browse course catalog
2. Select courses/packages
3. Proceed to checkout
4. Payment processing
5. Account creation/enrollment
